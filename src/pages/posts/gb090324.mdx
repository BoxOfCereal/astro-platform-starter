### Features
- 3 Drum samples
- 3 Instrument samples
- 3 Drumloop samples
- 1 music loop
- 3 step volume control
- kill switch

But the most interesting one is a _record_ and _playback_ that seems to be able to record The sequence of Key presses for a bar and play it back.

### Quirks

#### Out of tune!

When I press the `C` key my tuner reads a detuned `B`. And when I press `C#` I get `C`, but it is out of tune by about `36 cents`.


#### Idle Jingle

If left alone for a while, the piano will play a Jingle and cut power to the speaker.
